Gandaki Technical Institude (GTI)

Gandaki Technical Institude (GTI) was established in 2068 B.S. GTI was formed under government policy for improved access to higher education. The main function of Gandaki Technical Institude is to produce skilled human resources necessary for national development by providing quality education. In order to achieve such an objective, it has advanced training for working in a variety of settings and an evaluation methodology with high priority given to practical knowledge and research.

We have as a mission to train competent professional teachers for all levels of education as well as conduct research, disseminate knowledge and contribute to educational policy and development
