Introduction

Gandaki Technical Institute (GTI)

Gandaki Technical Institute is a distinct technical college affiliated to CTEVT and recognized by respective council. Gandaki Technical Institute is managed by academicians, researchers and prominent professional with extensive exposure to a wide range of knowledge, experience and teaching/ learning practices from 2052 B.S. as Argakhachi Technical School, Argakhachi and form 2068 B. S. as Gandaki Technical Institute, Pokhara till 2078 B. S. which then both colleges got consolidated and emerged as new Gandaki Technical Institute, Pokhara- 10 with handful of vision and goals for the purpose of providing quality, practical and real life based technical education and aim of being a center of excellence through quality education, research and services.

GTI is devoted to produce skillful, market oriented and competent graduate who can contribute in delivering the technical services effectively for this GTI has been implementing latest teaching and learning methodologies to meet global standard along with tranquil educational environment. Therefore, GTI might be one of the best destinations for carrying an outstanding knowledge and experiences in the field of technical education for every interested Nepalese student.
