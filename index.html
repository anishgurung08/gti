Greetings from Gandaki Technical Institute Pokhara (GTI)

We are confident that if you choose to complete one of our health related degrees with us, you will be aptly prepared to begin your journey in a health professions career. Our School is committed to providing a values-based quality educational experience for skilled, entry-level health professionals.

Our faculty and staff strive to fulfill the principles of Abraham Lincoln's life through the one health philosophy of linking public health, animal welfare and environmental health.
