Greetings from Gandaki Technical Institute Pokhara (GTI)

We are confident that if you choose to complete one of our health related degrees with us, you will be aptly prepared to begin your journey in a health professions career. Our School is committed to providing a values-based quality educational experience for skilled, entry-level health professionals.

Our faculty and staff strive to fulfill the principles of Abraham Lincoln's life through the one health philosophy of linking public health, animal welfare and environmental health.

Our Educational Programs

Various educational program run by us.

PCL In General Medicine(HA)

The primary focus of the professional curriculum of the College of Diploma In General Medicine(HA) is the three years (3 years), full-time academic and clinical degree program leading to the education that includes leadership skills,communications and community service.

Certificate In Medical Laboratory Technology(CMLT)

Certificate In Medical Laboratory Technology(CMLT) is a healthcare career that combines advanced laboratory science with modern medical care and produce advanced skills necessary to deliver high quality health services needed for country.

Pre-Diploma In Veterinary Medicine(VJTA)

The veterinary curriculum has been designed with the end in mind - to prepare the ideal graduates of tomorrow. As such, the curriculum is designed to be both horizontally and vertically integrated, i.e., as often as possible material taught in a given semester is aligned.
