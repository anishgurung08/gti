Message From Chairman

Gandaki Technical College has been established with the objective of providing quality technical manpower to the entire country by providing quality technical manpower and supporting nation building by providing quality education.

Yes, we have been running Arghakhanchi Technical School Pvt. Ltd. since 2052 and Gandaki Technical Institute Pvt. Ltd. in Pokhara metropolis since 2068. With the affiliation of CTEVT, we were conducting various technical educations in various fields of health and non-health, from a short period of 3 months to a 3-year diploma. Both of these organizations were merged in 2078 BS and named as Gandaki Technical Institute Pvt. We have moved forward with a new plan by further refining this organization from the long experience of running the organization for the last 27 years.

In this organization, we have given top priority to the quality of education and built the structure of the organization accordingly. You have also adopted a completely modern method of educational study method. We have managed experienced training for teaching. We have also managed a lot of educational materials. We have built various modern laboratories for experimental practice. We have established a digital library. We have managed separate hostels for students in the institution.

Arrangements have been made to conduct OJT at Gandaki Medical College and Teaching Hospital in Pokhara. Similarly, for the experimental practice of other subjects, we have also managed in various reputed institutes. Adequate playgrounds such as football, volleyball, badminton, table tennis and other materials have been provided to the students for extra-curricular activities. In addition, the entire necessary infrastructure has been constructed for the students. We are always committed to providing quality education by operating our educational institution in a full family environment, respecting the spirit of the students

Sincerely,
Dhruba Sharma
Chairman
