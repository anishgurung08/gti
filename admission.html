Admission Procedure

Interested students who have completed or appeared their secondary final exams can directly apply to the college with their academic certificates, citizenship certificate or passport or birth certificate or citizenship of either of the parents. The students awaiting results can get provisional admissions. Once at the college, the admission team will assess the entry requirement mentioned below and assist in admission process. Applicants should be minimum 17 years of age and should possess the minimum qualifications mentioned here under.

Diploma In General Medicine(HA)

Academic Level

  • Completion of SEE Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 55% or 2.2 CGPA*
  • Physically and mentally fit
  • Must Pass the Entrance Examination conducted by CTEVT

Diploma In Laboratory Technician(CMLT)

Academic Level

  • Completion of SEE Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 55% or 2.2 CGPA*
  • Physically and mentally fit
  • Must Pass the Entrance Examination conducted by CTEVT

Pre-Diploma In Veterinary Medicine(VJTA)

Academic Level

  • Completion of SEE Qualification with an aggregate ≥ 55% or 2.2 CGPA*
  • Physically and mentally fit
  • Must Pass the Entrance Examination conducted by CTEVT
