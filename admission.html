Admission Process

Admission Procedure

Students who have passed SLC/SEE are eligible for the following Programs:

  1. HA
  2. CMLT
  3. VJTA

Academic certificate, Citizenship certificate or underaged child recommendation certificate and recently taken passport size photo are needed to fill up the form. Those who passed the entrance examination taken by the CTEVT are admitted by the academy on the Merit basis.

Admission Requirement

Each interested students has to submit an application form with all the necessary information mention in the form. Following documents are mandatory.

  • Required application form fee
  • Four recent passport (colour) sizes photographs
  • Certified photocopy of SLC/SEE
  • Certified photocopy of the citizenship Certificate

Admission Process

  • The Entrance exam will be conducted by CTEVT and will cover English, Math and Science of SLC/SEE level.
  • The application form for the entrance exam will be made avaliabe in Gandaki Technical Institute.
  • HA, CMLT and VJTA will have the total capacity of 40 seats per year.
  • Those top 37 candidates able to maintain their position on the merit based result will able to join the courses. The candidate with the Highest Marks in Entrance Exam conducted by CTEVT for GTI will got Merit Scholarship for one year. Similarly merit scholarship is given to the student who secure highest position in first and second year respectively. 3 seats are allocated for classified scholarship.
  • Fee structure, admission fee will be according to the rules and regulation of CTEVT and hostel charge fixed according to rules of GTI.
